Who is Jerry?

Hey, I am Jerry and I am a freelance artist. I love music, from classical to modern rap, and I have been practicing piano and guitar for as long as I can remember, but only recently have I realized how much of an impact it can have on people and on myself. You see, being an artist is a bit like being a writer. You have to find your voice, make things that matter to you, and then share them with the world. There is no one-size-fits-all approach to art; there is just YOU! And by sharing your work, you are helping other people find out what they want out of their lives, too.

I love music because it lets me share my deepest thoughts and ideas with others in a way that connects us all in a way no one else can do. It iss like making connections with strangers—but more personal!
